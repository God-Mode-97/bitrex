About Us



BITREX'17



Creation, Learning and Experience. With these goals the Department of Computer Science, BHU presents BITREX'17 annual Technical Fest of Institute of Science, Banaras Hindu University. BITREX is an endeavour to establish a technical cult and create tech-friendly enviornment to unveil new talents and glorify the ones already existing.
BITREX is a platform to dig deeper into technology and provide a stage to veterans where they can claim their kingship through various competitions, aspire for an enviornment where individuals can enhance their skills and take a closer look at different arenas of the tech world through workshops, learn from some eminent personalities through talks and above all use their knowledge and skills of this discipline to inspire and work for a better tomorrow.
